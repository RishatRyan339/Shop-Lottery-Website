About the Website

Shopper Lottery is Malta’s first community shopping lottery, giving consumers the opportunity to win tens of thousands of Euros in vouchers every year!

This community lottery brings together businesses from all sectors under one customer loyalty umbrella.


The hundreds of organisations taking part in this scheme offer products and services everybody needs as we go about our daily lives – from shops selling the basics like bread and milk, to beauticians, restaurants, white good outlets and kitchen suppliers.


You name it and Shopper Lottery has it covered. (And if we don’t, let us know and we will try and get that sorted!).


The beautiful thing for you, the consumer, is that you don’t need to do anything special. Just make your purchase as normal and then digitally send us your receipts. For every €25 you spend at one of our participating outlets, you get one ticket.


That ticket is then valid for every draw during the whole year (from when it is issued) giving you many, many chances to win!


Every week we will be sending out vouchers to 26 different winners ranging in value from €250 to €25 – helping hundreds and hundreds of people every year make essential or desirable purchases.


So what are you waiting for? Start buying from one of our participating outlets today and get your receipts sent in straight away
